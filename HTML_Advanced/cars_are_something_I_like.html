Cars: Something I Like

Cars are one of my favorite things. I love how they look, sound, and drive. Whether the car is fast, or old and vintage, or even high-tech cars today, each one is interesting. Cars represent freedom and adventure. It's also fun to learn about how they work and all the new technology being added to them.
